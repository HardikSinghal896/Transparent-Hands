Introduction Among the many wonders of the 21st century, the remarkable revolution in diagnostic testing stands out in the healthcare sector. Most sensitive reagent kits, machines that can process hundreds of samples at a time, and highly skilled technicians at work have completely changed the dynamics of laboratory testing.
